The Story of Berk

In the Viking village of Berk, young Hiccup defies tradition when he befriends a feared dragon, the Night Fury. Together with his dragon Toothless, he discovers that dragons are not the enemies his people believe them to be, but loyal companions who can change the future of their world.

MOVIES

How To Train Your Dragon (2010)

Hiccup befriends Toothless and changes the relationship between Vikings and dragons forever.

How To Train Your Dragon 2 (2014)

Hiccup and Toothless discover an ice cave and face a powerful dragon master threatening their peace.

How To Train Your Dragon: The Hidden World (2019)

As danger mounts, Hiccup and Toothless search for a hidden dragon utopia before enemies find them.

How To Train Your Dragon: Homecoming (2019)

A holiday special where Vikings honor their bond with dragons and Toothless reunites with Hiccup’s family.
